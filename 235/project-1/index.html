BAKING

YEAR-ROUND TREATS

Chocolate chip cookies are

a sweet delight.

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup butter partially melted
    • 3/4 cup packed light brown sugar
    • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • 1 large egg
    • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

  • First, Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    In a medium bowl mix together the flour, baking soda, and salt before setting aside.
    Next, In a large bowl beat together the butter, light brown and granulated sugar, and the vanila extract until just smooth. Beat in the egg just until combined.

  • Mix the flour mixture into the wet mixture until it is properly combined, then stir in your chocolate chips.
    Using an ice cream scoop or a large spoon, drop cookies about 2-3 inches apart onto your cookie sheet.
    Finally, bake for 8-10 minutes. When they are done, they will look light brown around the edges and underdone in the center. Remove from the oven and Allow them to sit on the sheet for 10 minutes before moving them to a cooling rack until fully cooled.

    After that, all that's left is to enjoy!

prep for later: this cookie dough can be frozen and defrosted if they are put into balls first. The dough takes overnight in the fridge to defrost, or a couple hours at room temperature.

If you don't like chocolate chips, they can be substituted easily. try mashed raspberries and white chocolate for something more fruity!
